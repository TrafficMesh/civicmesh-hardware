# Raspberry Pi CM4 GPIO Pinout

## Camera Interface
- MIPI CSI-2: Camera connector

## OBD-II
- UART0 (pins 8, 10): OBD interface

## GPS
- UART1 (pins 27, 28): u-blox NEO-M9N

## Cellular Modem
- USB via carrier board hub

## Power Management
- GPIO17: Battery low signal
- GPIO27: Buck converter enable
