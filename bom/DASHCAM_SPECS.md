# Dashcam Specifications

- Model: Viofo A119
- Resolution: 1440p @ 30fps
- Field of View: 120°
- Sensor: 1/2" CMOS
- Lens: f/1.6 aperture
- Video Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 8-10 Mbps (target)
- Storage: 128GB microSD

## Calibration
- Intrinsic calibration (focal length, principal point, distortion)
- Extrinsic calibration (pixel -> real-world 3D mapping)
