# Power Management Design

## Input
- OBD-II 12V (vehicle)

## Processing
- Buck converter: 12V -> 5V @ 3A
- Thermal analysis: <65°C under full load

## Battery Backup
- Li-ion UPS: 10Ah
- Runtime: 15-18 hours
- Auto-switchover: <5ms

## Protection
- Polarity protection
- Fuses: 10A input, 5A output
